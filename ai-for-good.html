AI for Good Programme

Free AI support for UK financial inclusion charities and credit unions.

A limited number of free consulting hours each year for credit unions, debt advice charities, and community lenders across the UK.

Why we do this

AI expertise, shared.

ShiftAi Systems Ltd builds AI systems for regulated UK banks and lenders. As part of that work, we offer a limited amount of free consulting time each year to mission-led organisations working in financial inclusion, debt advice, and community lending.

Who this is for

Built for the front line of financial exclusion.

Credit unions, debt advice charities, and community lenders operating in the UK, particularly organisations supporting people who are financially excluded or underserved by mainstream lending.

What this includes

Practical help. Not a sales pitch.

  • Document and application processing review
  • Reducing administrative workload through AI-assisted workflows
  • Internal knowledge and policy search tools
  • Affordability assessment workflow support
  • AI governance and responsible adoption guidance

How this works

Free. Transparent. No surprises.

This programme is offered at no cost, with no commercial obligation. Alongside the charitable purpose, occasional projects from this programme may be referenced as case studies in our portfolio, with the organisation's agreement. We will always ask first and nothing is published without consent.
